                      shayneo • 2 years ago

                      The camera movement can be a bit jerky when its mapped straight to a key up or down. What I found was *much* smoother is to keep a delta_x and delta_y variable, usually set to zero, then when the key DOWN event happens set it to + or - some smaller number (perhaps using the timing delta to facto in FPS) and then on the render loop translating it then. Finally on the key UP even, setting it to zero. That means it smoothly scrolls when the key is down, but stops when the finger is lifted. I presume a similar scheme could be used for touch screen events.
